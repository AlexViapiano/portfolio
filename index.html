Hi! My name is Alex Viapiano and I am a full-stack web developer from Montreal, Canada.

I have a deep curiosity and passion for existing and emerging web technologies. Specializing in JavaScipt and React.js, I can effectively produce high quality and responsive web apps, mobile apps and landing pages. As a freelancer, my clients range from individuals to small start-ups such as GOrendezvous, to large enterprise corporations such as Nuance Communications. Being highly attentive to detail and having an unequivocal love for creation and design, I specialize in building products specific to the business needs of my clients. My mission statement is to simplify and improve lives.
